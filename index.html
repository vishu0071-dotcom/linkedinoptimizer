FAQ

Frequently asked questions about AI resume optimization

What is an ATS and why does my resume need to pass it?

ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System — software used by 98% of Fortune 500 companies and most Indian MNCs to automatically screen resumes before a human ever reads them. If your resume isn't keyword-optimized for the specific job, it gets rejected automatically. ResumeAI rewrites your resume to pass ATS filters for any job description.

Is ResumeAI free to use?

Yes! ResumeAI gives you 1 free optimization when you sign up. After that, credits start at just ₹99 for 5 optimizations. There are no subscriptions — buy credits only when you need them.

How does the AI resume optimizer work?

You paste your existing resume and the job description you're targeting. ResumeAI's AI analyzes job requirements, identifies missing keywords and skills, rewrites your resume to match the role, and gives you an ATS score from 0–100. The whole process takes under 45 seconds.

Does ResumeAI work for Indian companies and MNCs?

Yes. ResumeAI works for roles at Infosys, TCS, Wipro, HCL, Accenture, Google India, Amazon India, Flipkart, Zomato, and all other companies that use ATS systems.

Can I optimize my LinkedIn profile too?

Yes! Every optimization includes a LinkedIn About section tailored to your target role. Copy-paste it into your LinkedIn profile to attract Indian recruiters searching for your skills.

Is my resume data safe and private?

Absolutely. Your resume is processed securely and never stored permanently or shared with third parties. We use Firebase and industry-standard encryption.
